About Me

Hi there! My name is Ishtanetra Siva, and I am a rising Year 10 student, passionate about science. I am also a published author! Find out more about it: here!.

In this website, I will detail my journey as a student, including my passion for science as well as my honours and achievements.

My Book

The Colours of Sabdas: An anthology of poems and short stories is a compilation of numerous stories and poems authored by me, ranging from the topic of travel, to science fiction to stories inspired by international mythology. The book is available for purchase on Kindle and also on paperback .

As a published author, I have been featured on Khaleej Times' 'Young Times' , a weekly newspaper column in the national magazine featuring the achievements and activities of young individuals.

Science

I am also very passionate about science and discovery. I have grown as a science communicator, having established my own blog and YouTube channel . Additionally, I am a Board Member and Chief Author of an international non-for-profit organisation known as Sciencious Co , where I write and produce monthly articles. Additionally, as a part of Sciencious' knowledge and outreach, I am a Podcast host for Sciencious Soundwaves . Here, we interview professors and researchers involved with research, and engage in scientific conversations.

Resume

Education

Al Yasmina Academy

2010-current

I am currently a Year 10 student in Al Yasmina Academy. I am taking the following GCSE subjects: Mathematics, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, English Literature, English Language, Arabic, Computer Science, Psychology and Art.
I am an active member of my school community, having been the AYA Green Ambassadors' (the Sustainability community of our school) Lead Representative and Website Manager. Additionally, I am the founder and leader of my school's TED-Ed Club, where we aim to give students the opportunity to give their own TED-style talk which will be officially presented by TED-Ed on their YouTube channel and website.

Experience

Sciencious

2021-ongoing

I work as an author for Sciencious, producing monthly articles. Additionally, I am a podcast host for Sciencious Soundwaves, an initiative by Sciencious where we interview professors and scientists involved in research.

TED-Ed Club: Founder and Leader

2021-ongoing

I created my school's TED-Ed Club, which is designed to empower individuals and raise their voices for causes they believe in. Our club is recognised by TED-Ed. Currently, I am leading a club of over 10+ individuals. Each week, I commit 3-4 hours a week for TED-Ed Club.

Lead Representative and Website Manager of AYA Green Ambassadors

2021-ongoing

I am the Lead Representative of Al Yasmina Academy's Sustainability Committee (Green Ambassadors). In total, I commit 2-3 hours a week. My roles have included being the website manager and heading the poster campaign outreach program, as I designed school-wide campaign posters. I supported the sustainability team in achieving the Eco-Schools 'Bronze Award' given by the Environmental Centre for Arab Towns as a part of their educational outreach.
